Conductive Education

Conductive Education is an amazing educational method that first of all considers every child as an individual then focuses on their disability. Conductive Education provides a holistic approach with in the rehabilitation and education process believing all areas of development are equally important and should be addressed and enhanced in the same time.

  • Get In Touch

The Conductive approach advocates and focus on developing self-esteem and self confidence. It is a complex educational and physical program that helps children develop independent skills, achieve the best possible mobility, assists children's speech development, will facilitate academic learning. The program has a strong focus on teaching parents and family members so the task-series can easily be followed up in the home environment, or be implemented in schools and childcare centers.

Conductive Education is a worldwide, well known system, with centers operating in all major cities. Information about Conductive Education is widely available on the internet.

Programs

Home visits/Individual conductive education session

Please contact Gabi for a home visit and assessment. The assessment will focus on the positives, will emphases existing skills and build on them. After the initial appointment my aim is to develop an individual program for your child, teach the family, childcare, school how to implement the program, and continue build skills with the help of weekly or fortnightly

Hydrotherapy

Hydrotherapy is a wonderful way to participate in a therapy session. It is relaxing, calming in the same time a very efficient and intense workout. Hydrotherapy sessions are conducted in a warm, heated pool, 33-35 degree temperature.Sessions can be organized at several publicly accessible locations.
Recommended session time: 30-45 minute. Hydrotherapy session can be provided weekly.

Family Counseling session

Family counseling sessions are available after the initial assessment. The session aims to provide practical, common sense approach to support family members during challenging times. It is one of the most difficult challenge in our life's when something traumatic happens to our precious children. I'm fully committed to assist all individuals towards self-knowlidge,self- discovery and help to find the way within to create and achieve a better tomorrow.

It will be an honor to share a fraction of time together and to share part of your journey.
